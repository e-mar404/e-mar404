It is surprisingly hard to consistently think of a common theme that is not
'programming' to name the of the weekly blog, sooooo I'll just call it media
recap \#number. My key take away from this week is very profound, I love
youtube :). Ok maybe not profound at all but I truly enjoy all the information

## Why Programmers adopt Bad Ideas - Casey Muratori
[Interview by NeetCode](https://youtu.be/Z8B4BSi35CI?si=HjEPkYv2abO4MIaY)

- math is cool
- a huge chunk of the interview is Casey talking about what he thinks is the 
  most interesting math/programming related problem he's encounter in his years
  and I love the way he explains things. Sometimes it feels a little slow but
  there is so much passion behind his words that makes me want to go out and
  learn more about what he is talking about
- very interesting to see Casey and NeetCode (sorry dont know his actual name)
  talk about their resistance to using AI
- also yes it is true, programmers adopt bad ideas because we are very
  opinionated individuals that are able to make those ideas work and become a 
  reality (at least within out heads and immediate environment, maybe not 
  reality in the global sense)
- real, webdev sucks, srry :P
